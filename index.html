A science fiction universe spanning 100,000 years. Two novels and sixteen short stories trace the collision between humanity and the alien Dyn — from deep prehistory to the far-future war against the Apathy, a force that may be killing us for our own good.

Story readers, tactical reconstructions, 3D models, and intelligence dossiers built around the source fiction. The original stories are published at ascentuniverse.wordpress.com.

What is the Listener?

Not a god. Not a machine. Not the ghost
of the Others, though it wears their silence
like a garment. It is the thing that waits
at the bottom of every well of loneliness —
the presence you feel when you are most certain
you are alone.

It chose Eszel not because she was broken
but because she was honest about the breaking.
It emerged from an explosion of light
at Kailash, where the mountain split open
and everything the universe had been hiding
poured through the crack.

I am everything, it said.
And for once, that was not a lie.
